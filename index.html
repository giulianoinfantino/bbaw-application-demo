About

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Stuttgart (since 2023). Recently, I was a Visiting Assistant in Research at Yale University and a Visiting Research Student at Stanford University. I study how thought and nature relate in Hegel, with attention to Kant and issues in modality, logic, and the philosophy of nature. I previously studied philosophy, classics, German, and comparative literature at the Universities of Cologne and Bonn.

Areas of competence. Metaphysics and Philosophy of Nature (Kant, Hegel).

Hegel Kant Modality Logic Nature
Research

Current project

My dissertation reconstructs Hegel’s philosophy of nature and argues that the existence of nature is constitutive for truth and falsity. The project addresses three questions: (i) What is thought? (ii) What is nature? (iii) How do thought and nature relate?
